Hilbert's First Axiom

Axiom I.1: For any two distinct points \( A \) and \( B \), there exists a line \( \ell \) such that \( A \in \ell \) and \( B \in \ell \).

For example: $$\exists \ell \text{ such that } A, B \in \ell$$
